The criteria

Who We're Looking For

We are not a training ground. We're looking for advisors who are already exceptional — or clearly on the path to becoming so.

We are looking for
Proven track record in UAE residential or commercial real estate — minimum 2 years of closed transactions
Existing client relationships and a professional network you can bring to a premium platform
An obsession with quality — in how you present yourself, your listings, and your client service
Ambition to operate in the AED 5M+ segment and genuine drive to build a high-value book of business
Absolute discretion and a reputation for integrity — non-negotiable at every level of our firm
Valid UAE RERA certification (or committed to obtaining it prior to joining)
This role is not for
Beginners looking for their first step into real estate — we are not a training academy
High-volume, low-ticket advisors who prioritise transaction count over client relationship quality
Advisors not yet committed to the Dubai market long-term or those seeking short-term opportunity
Anyone whose personal conduct, online presence, or professional reputation doesn't align with a luxury brand
